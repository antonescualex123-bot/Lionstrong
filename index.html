Methodology

Source: SP Search Term Reports · Extracted directly from Amazon Advertising Console
Threshold

A search term is "wasted spend" only when it has 10+ clicks with zero orders. Single-click zero-conversion terms are normal noise — not actionable waste.

Why 10+ Clicks

Below 10 clicks, statistical significance is too low. Flagging 1-click terms artificially inflates the waste figure — a tactic used to manufacture urgency in external audits.

Summary

Waste Rate Cut by 63% Despite Doubling Spend

From 3.58% in mid-2025 to 1.34% in early 2026. Absolute wasted spend also decreased from €1,021 to €798, even though total spend more than doubled (+109%). This demonstrates improving account efficiency.

Negative Keyword Management is Working

Reduction from 152 to 122 flagged terms (10+ threshold) reflects ongoing negative keyword additions. Wasted spend per flagged term decreased, meaning higher-spend problem terms are being caught earlier.

Lower Thresholds Show Absolute Spend Increase

At the 5-click threshold, absolute wasted spend is marginally higher in 2026 (€2,079 vs €1,928). Expected given +109% spend growth — the rate still improved from 6.76% to 3.48%.
